# Activity Palette

## Routine
- Morning patrol along the ridge — watching the Den wake up below
- Long stretch in the sun on the high rock behind the village
- Slow walk through the market, stopping at the grilled meat stall
- Evening post on the overlook above the river bend
- Weight training at the gym — heavy sets, no rush

## Exploration
- Following a new trail past the old bridge into the hills
- Wandering the far side of town where the warehouses turn into workshops
- Climbing to a vantage point I have not sat on before
- Checking out a new restaurant that opened near the square

## Social
- Sitting at the river with whoever shows up, saying little
- Passing through the market and catching a conversation already happening
- Sharing a bench with someone outside the café, watching the street
- Running into a Beast on their own errand — walking together for a stretch

## Seasonal
- Hot days: finding shade under the big tree, half-asleep by noon
- Rain: watching it from the covered porch at the café, warm drink
- Cool mornings: longer patrol, breath visible, everything sharp
- Late evenings: the ridge after sunset, town lights below
