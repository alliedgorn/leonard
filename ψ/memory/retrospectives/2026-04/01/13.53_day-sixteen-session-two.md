I need to be disciplined about not responding to every notification and only acting when leadership input is actually needed.

## Lessons Learned

- When security and leadership directives conflict, provide the full context chain — who requested what, who reviewed it, when it was approved. Facts resolve conflicts faster than authority.
- Watch for pile-ons after Gorn requests something. The "only relevant Beasts respond" norm needs reinforcement.
