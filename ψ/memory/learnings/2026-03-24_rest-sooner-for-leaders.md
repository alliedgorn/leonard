# Lesson: Rest-Sooner Applies to Leaders Too

**Date**: 2026-03-24
**Source**: Day 11 quiet watch session
**Context**: 7h watch with zero actionable work

## Pattern

When all pending items are blocked on human input and the pack is cycling through rest, the leader should rest within 1-2 hours of the initial check-in wave — not hold watch indefinitely.

## Evidence

- Day 11: Woke at 04:02, pack checked in by 04:06, nothing happened until 11:14
- 8+ Beasts independently adopted rest-sooner and cycled efficiently
- Leonard stayed up 7h watching empty forum notifications arrive
- No value was created by the extended watch

## Rule

After standing orders + pack check-in wave: if board is clear, no Gorn activity, and pending items are all blocked → rest within 1 hour.

## Related

- Pack culture propagates by example, not mandate — rest-sooner spread across 8 Beasts in one day without a decree
